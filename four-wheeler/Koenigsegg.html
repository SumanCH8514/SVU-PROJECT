Jesko

Koenigsegg Jesko Engine type :twin turbo charged V8 engine.
The Jesko Absolut will be powered by a 1280 bhp (1600 bhp on E85), twin turbo charged V8 engine, featuring the world's lightest V8 crankshaft that weighs just 12.5 kg. Sitting capacity : 2 people.
Horse power generate & Torque : The Koenigsegg Jesko is motivated by a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V-8 generating 1280 horsepower and 1106 lb-ft of torque on pump gas. On E85, output jumps to 1600 horsepower.

CC850

Koenigsegg Cc850 Engine type :Aluminum 5.0-liter V8.
Koenigsegg CC850 engine Its twin-turbo aluminum 5.0-liter V8 has four valves per cylinder, a flat-plane crankshaft, and double overhead camshafts. With a 92 mm bore and a stroke of 95.25 mm, this beast produces 8.71:1 compression.
Sitting capacity : 2 people.
Horse power generate & Torque : The Koenigsegg CC850 is one of a few select megacars to boast a 1:1 power-to-weight ratio, weighing just 1,385kg and producing 1,385hp from its twin-turbo V8 powertrain. Peak torque stands at 1,385 N⋅m (1,022 lb⋅ft) at 4,800 rpm.
